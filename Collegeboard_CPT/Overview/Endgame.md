# ♟ Endgame strategy

The endgame represents the final phase of the match where the departure of major pieces transforms the king from a liability into a potent offensive asset. Success in this stage requires absolute technical precision, as players must master the art of pawn promotion while utilizing their remaining forces to create inescapable mating nets. A superior understanding of theoretical endgames often allows a player to convert a drawish position into a definitive victory through superior piece coordination and calculation.
