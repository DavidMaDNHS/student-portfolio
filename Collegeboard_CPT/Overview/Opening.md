# ♟ Opening strategy
The Opening module focuses on establishing a strong foundation by controlling the center and developing pieces efficiently in the first phase of the game. Students will study world-class systems such as the Italian Game, the Sicilian Defense, and the Queen's Gambit to understand the specific goals behind each move. The curriculum emphasizes essential tactics like early king safety (castling), avoiding premature queen attacks, and identifying "traps" that can end a match before it truly begins.
