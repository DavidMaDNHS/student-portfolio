# ♟ Overview

This interactive chess academy is designed to provide a structured learning path for players of all skill levels, from beginners to experts. Users can register and log in to access specialized training modules covering everything from basic piece movement to advanced endgame strategies. By selecting a personalized skill level, the tool tailors its curriculum to ensure every student finds the right challenge to improve their game.
