# ♟ Midgame Strategy
The Midgame module transitions from development to active warfare, focusing on the complex art of piece coordination and positional planning. You will learn to identify critical tactical patterns like forks, pins, and skewers to win material and disrupt your opponent's rhythm. The curriculum emphasizes the long-term strategy of pawn structures, teaching you how to create weaknesses in the enemy camp while securing your own king for the final push.
